Start with 2 position point and mow down all enemies in the map for extra budget and polymers for upgrades. Find the objects and unlock more areas as you go do some cleaning the Mercenary Complex interriors. Beware the timer will start after you choose the position point. The longer you take, the powerful enemy waves will arrive. So be prepared for the worst scenario.

Hint:

You do not need to complete the level 100%, because you can go anywhere. So good luck! It' best to upgrade all the characters weapons and research all technologies before you go on this map.

Story/Plot			: 

Classified location on Milky Way Galaxy; Kelkron 11 Star System ; Planet: Kelkron 11; 

(Alliance Military Complex + Mikko Sandt Corps + Solar System Expedition X 101st Galactic Cluster Mercenary "Secilia Front" + 423rd Auxillary Artillery)

During the search for the Mandala Device, a group of AMC explorers have detected a signal that is not on the hyper decoder sensor.  A group of recon units have scouted the star system only a habitable planet with vast and hospitable areas. Kelkron 11's abundant resources will allow AMC to expand territory and military strength. Somehow they manage to locate a signal where it is originated. AMC recon units discovers a military forces are involved on the planet surface. Among to their discovery, they learn that military is not originated from the Milky Way Galaxy. However, they encountered the unknown military forces settled from another universe. Unfortunately, AMC expedition reports a unknown forces stationed at large complex with advance defensive structures. The expedition crew knew that taking on alone can prove to be more challenging. AMC spaceship crew have manage to contact Earth Assault Forces to assist on taking on the Mercenary Complex. However, their available strength may not able to outmatch the mercenarys strength because of their apex technology. Solar System forces planned out that the capture of their technology will be vital to survive the outsiders onslaught. Although, they knew that the battle will prove more difficult if the Mercenary legions able to contact their reinforcements. Can the Solar System forces defeat their infamous Merecenary forces and claim the planet as a colony? Or leave it alone to wait for their next long-term galactic warfare.
